# 📊 E-Commerce Revenue Optimization & Business Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end E-Commerce Analytics solution built using PostgreSQL, Python, and Power BI. The objective was to analyze business performance, evaluate customer behavior, and derive actionable insights to improve revenue and retention.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

- Analyze overall revenue performance
- Measure customer churn
- Segment customers based on lifetime value
- Identify high-value customers
- Build an executive dashboard for decision-making

---

## 🗂 Dataset

- Source: Olist E-Commerce Public Dataset  
- Records: 100K+ transactions  
- Data includes customers, orders, and payments information  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

- **PostgreSQL** – Database design & SQL querying  
- **Python (Pandas, SQLAlchemy)** – Data processing & CLV calculation  
- **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard development  
- **Git & GitHub** – Version control  

---

## 🏗 Project Architecture
- Raw CSV Data → PostgreSQL → SQL KPIs → Python Analysis → Power BI Dashboard


---

## 📊 Key KPIs Developed

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Customer Churn Rate (6-month inactivity definition)
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Repeat vs One-Time Customers

---

## 👥 Customer Analysis

- Calculated CLV per customer
- Segmented customers into:
  - Low Value
  - Medium Value
  - High Value
- Identified revenue concentration among top-tier customers

---

## 📉 Churn Analysis

- Defined churn as no purchase within 6 months
- Observed high proportion of one-time buyers
- Highlighted need for customer retention strategies

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Executive Overview
- Revenue KPIs
- Orders & AOV
- Monthly revenue trend
- Churn rate

### 🔹 Customer Insights
- CLV segmentation
- Revenue by state
- Top 10 cities
- Repeat vs One-time customers

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- A small percentage of high-value customers generate majority revenue.
- Majority of customers are one-time buyers.
- Retention strategies could significantly improve revenue stability.
- Revenue shows seasonal fluctuations across months.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Focus retention campaigns on high CLV customers
- Launch re-engagement campaigns for churned users
- Strengthen loyalty programs
- Expand marketing efforts in top-performing cities
